Linea Bar is a concept-driven space where design, atmosphere, and experience come together seamlessly. Built around the idea of minimal elegance and refined simplicity, Linea Bar offers more than just drinks—it creates a mood, a rhythm, and a place to connect. Every detail, from the interior architecture to the curated menu, reflects a balance between modern aesthetics and timeless comfort. The space is designed to feel both sophisticated and welcoming, making it ideal for casual meetings, creative conversations, or quiet moments of escape. Linea Bar represents a vision of contemporary urban lifestyle—where quality, design, and experience are at the core. This project showcases not only a brand identity, but also a digital and visual concept developed as part of a creative portfolio.
